Privacy Policy

Palm Beach Pete Clicker

Last updated: March 31, 2026

This policy describes how information is handled when you use the mobile application Palm Beach Pete Clicker (the “App”) on Apple iOS. The App is provided by the developer identified on the App’s App Store product page (“we,” “us,” “our”).

Privacy contact: Use the support URL or developer website shown on the App Store listing, or email mason_mcfalda@icloud.com.

Summary

  • We do not operate user accounts, login, or our own cloud servers for game progress in this App.
  • Game progress, settings, and purchase flags are stored on your device using Apple’s standard local storage (UserDefaults), unless you use iCloud/device backup, in which case Apple’s backup rules apply.
  • In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit.
  • Optional rewarded video ads are provided by Google through the Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob). When ads run, Google (not us) may collect or process data under Google’s policies to deliver and measure advertising.

1. Information the App stores on your device (local only)

The App saves gameplay and preferences on your iPhone using Apple’s on-device storage (e.g. cash, upgrades, settings, and whether certain purchases were completed). This data is not sent to servers we operate for gameplay purposes. If you delete the App or erase the device, that local data is removed unless restored from your backup.

2. Apple and in-app purchases (StoreKit)

When you buy or restore digital items in the App (for example shekel packs or a permanent “no ads” unlock), Apple processes payment and entitlement. We do not receive your full payment card number. Apple may process purchase-related data according to Apple’s Privacy Policy.

3. Google advertising (Google Mobile Ads / AdMob)

The App includes the Google Mobile Ads SDK to show optional rewarded video advertisements when you choose to watch an ad for in-game rewards. You are not required to watch ads to open the App.

Google may automatically collect or process technical and advertising-related information when the SDK loads or serves ads. Categories Google associates with this type of SDK commonly include, for example: device identifiers, coarse location, product interaction (ad impressions, clicks), diagnostics/performance, and advertising data, used for purposes such as third-party advertising, analytics, and developer advertising, as described in Google’s documentation and privacy materials.

We do not control Google’s data practices in detail. Please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services

You can limit ad tracking where Apple provides controls (Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, and related options on your device) and use Google’s ad settings and industry opt-outs as Google describes.

Note: The App’s source code does not integrate separate third-party analytics SDKs (for example Firebase Analytics) or social logins. Aside from Apple’s systems and Google’s ad SDK as described above, we do not embed additional advertising or analytics networks in the App.

4. Information we do not intentionally collect from you directly

We do not:

  • Require registration, a username, or an email to play
  • Operate a feature in the App that sends your contacts, photos, microphone, or camera to us
  • Knowingly sell personal information as a data broker

If you email us for support, we receive only what you put in that message.

5. Children

The App is a casual game and is not specifically designed for children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information in a way that concerns you, contact us using the privacy contact above and we will work with you in good faith to address it.

6. International users; your choices

If you are in the European Economic Area, the UK, or other regions with privacy laws, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of personal data, or to object to certain processing. Much of the data involved in this App is local to your device or held by Apple or Google. For data held by those companies, you may need to exercise rights directly with them using their tools and policies.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. The Last updated date will change when we do. If changes are material, we may also provide notice in the App or on the App Store description when practical.
